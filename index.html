"You're not prepared to deal with the truth...are you?"

A mysterious golden letter is sent to a select number of people... Its contents are unknown.

I Kat is a short, unconventional RPG horror game in which there is always another way. Solve puzzles, make friends (or not), and guide Kat as she attempts to uncover the mystery behind a strange golden letter.

This is a story-based puzzle game where each puzzle has multiple solutions and outcomes! There is always another way. The game is playable in about 45 minutes and contains multiple endings depending on your actions.

This is the prologue to Paper Lily, the full game (currently in development), but it's also a standalone story that can be enjoyed on its own.


Warning:

May contain flashing lights, some blood and unsettling scenes. Proceed at your own risk. Just kidding it's really not that bad. But still it's a horror game :)

ABOUT I KAT

Discover

Unearth the secrets of an ever-shifting forest while trying desperately not to die. An atmospheric open-world Quiet Forest like. Explore an ancient, ruined forest with nothing but a sword and the clothes on your back (and maybe a bunch of volatile and destructive bows).

Low System

Since the game has a very low system, it can be played easily on almost many computers.

Have Fun

Enjoy the lack of obstructions on your way, except about a trillion territorial cycloptic mages who will repeatedly pepper you with various explosive spells. Socialize with a cacophony of incredibly joyous and friendly characters, none of whom posses crippling depression from living in a world that is falling apart at the seams.
